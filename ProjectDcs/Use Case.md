## UC-FA-RE-001: Post Job via FA
**Actor**: HR Manager (FA User)

**FA-Specific Flow**:
1. Navigate: HR > Recruitment > New Position
2. Enter position details (FA form)
3. Post to job boards
4. Track applicants in FA

## UC-FA-RE-002: New Hire to HRM
**Actor**: HR Manager

**FA-Specific Flow**:
1. Offer accepted
2. Creates employee in ksf_FA_HRM
3. Triggers ksf_FA_Onboarding
4. Updates position status
